Sammy Whitby Photography, Your Day, Your Way

Welcome to Sammy Whitby Photography, I'm Sammy a professional photographer based in Bolton-Le-Sands, Lancashire. I cover all diffrent types of projects in Lancashire, Lake District and the whole of the North West. Have a look around and find out all about me, have a look at some of my work and read more about my wedding photography package here. My style is natural, modern and informal photography that reflects who you are as a couple, telling the story of your Event/day as it unfolds. I love my work as a lancashire photographer and would love to be a part of your special day.
